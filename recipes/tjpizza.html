Trader Joes Pizza!

Description

Making pizza can be difficult and time consuming. This Trader Joe pizza will be you weeknight dinner savior.

Ingredient List

  • Trader Joe pre-made pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce (your choice)
  • Red Onion
  • Garlic
  • Low moisture mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Chili Flakes

Directions

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 475 degrees
  2. Place dough on lightly floured pan and let sit for 30 minutes
  3. Peel then mince the garlic
  4. peel and cut onions into thin half circles
  5. Cut all your pepporonis into 4 pieces
  6. Place dough on oiled pan and gently flatten into a circle or the shape of your pan
  7. Spread tomato sauce on dough
  8. Add some of your minced garlic (save some for later)
  9. Add mozzarella cheese
  10. Add your cut pepporonis
  11. Add chili flakes
  12. Spread the rest of your garlic over the pizza
  13. Add your chopped onion to your pizza
  14. Place pizza in the oven for 15 minutes
  15. After 15 minute, set oven to broil
  16. Once the crust starts to brown, turn off the oven and pull the pizza out
